Welcome to [The Table]!

[The Table] is an augmented reality experience created by Georgia Tech's Augmented Reality lab. Add shapes and models to the world around you to create your own unique works of art. Collaborate with others to join the conversation! The mobile experience allows you to look through your phone and edit a location on site, while this desktop version enables you to choose your own location and edit it from anywhere.
